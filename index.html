From Briefcases to Bots: The Evolution of Consulting

Consulting has always been about solving problems. But the tools and methods? They’ve changed. Here's how we got here:

1950s–70s

Management consultants with clipboards and theory-based reports.

1980s–90s

ERP systems and process improvement methodologies like Six Sigma.

2000s

BI dashboards, Excel mastery, cloud starts to emerge.

2010s

Agile methodologies, SaaS proliferation, and digital tools speed things up.

2020s

RPA and task automation gain traction. AI enters the picture.
